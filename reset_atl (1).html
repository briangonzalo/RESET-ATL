What Is Reset

Not a gym. Not a spa.
Something Atlanta has never had.

Reset is an exclusive private club where professionals train, recover, work, and connect under one roof. Every detail — from the contrast therapy suites to the restaurant and member programming — is built around one idea: you perform at your best when recovery is non-negotiable.

Recovery Suite
Cold plunge, infrared and Finnish dry sauna, steam, contrast therapy, and red light. Built for daily use, not occasional visits.
Performance Floor
Premium strength and functional training, golf simulators, personal training, and golf-specific fitness programming.
Wellness Services
On-site IV therapy, massage, and curated wellness partners — all booked seamlessly through the club.
Restaurant & Bar
A full kitchen and bar for daily dining, post-workout meals, and evenings spent with people worth knowing.
Work Spaces
Private offices, meeting rooms, and high-speed connectivity for the professional who needs more than a coffee shop.
Member Network
Curated events, programming, and a membership built so the people inside are as valuable as the amenities.
